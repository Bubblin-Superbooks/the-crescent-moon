At length Eva asked: “Mr. M‘Crae, what’s the matter with you?”

He said: “Nothing.” And there followed another long silence.

Then, without a word of introduction, he began talking to her about his childhood. A long and disordered story. He didn’t seem to be considering her at all in the recitation. She might not even have been there, she thought, if it had not been that his eyes were always on her.
