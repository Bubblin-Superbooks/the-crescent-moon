Reading alternate verses they recited a psalm of David, a passionate song against idolaters; and a little of the passion came through into the voice of James, so that he spoke less precisely than usual, like a peasant of Far Forest, forgetting the accent which the training college had taught him. His voice rose and fell and echoed in the little church:

“Insomuch that they worshipped their idols, which turned to their own decay; yea, they offered their sons and their daughters unto devils.” p. 176And she heard herself reply:
